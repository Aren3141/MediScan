# MediScan AI

> AI in Healthcare. Redefined.

MediScan is a premium platform exploring the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and rapid healthcare diagnostics.

### Sprint 1: Genesis (Completed)

### Sprint 3: Experience (Upcoming)
